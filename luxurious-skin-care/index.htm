Discover New, Luxurious Skin Care from Around the World

Cynthia Akani

October 12, 2022 - 5 minute read

The skin is the largest organ of the human body. It covers the entire surface of our body and protects us from harmful external factors.

Composed of several layers, the skin uses each layer to perform unique functions.

The outermost layer of our skin is called the epidermis. This acts as a barrier between our body and the external environment.

The second layer is called dermis. This layer contains melanocytes (pigment-producing cells).

Melanocytes produce melanin, a brownish pigment that gives colour to the skin, hair, and eyes as well as protects the skin against UV radiation from the sun. Variation in the amount and kinds of melanin that melanocytes produce is the reason behind varying skin colours among humans.

The third layer of the skin is called hypodermis or subcutaneous tissue. It contains fat cells that produce ceramide, a lipid substance that helps protect our skin from water loss during bathing or sweating.

Often, people go all out in search of the best skin care product in the world, but there is no single best product out there. Each person has different needs and preferences when it comes to their beauty regimen. The same goes for products from different countries.

From the most famous brands in the world — Olay, Clinique, Oriflame, and more — to less-known but equally effective products, any skin care product is a tool on a mission and will best suit a particular skin composition.
