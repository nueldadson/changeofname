Frequently Asked Questions

There are two ways to obtain a court affidavit in Nigeria.

First, you may visit a court of law or notary public near you to have your affidavit sworn.

Second, you may use the online service of select Nigerian courts to obtain your affidavit.
The streamlined process involves that you: complete an online form, securely make payment, download/print an electronic copy of the affidavit that will be sent to your email address (within minutes), then sign the document as a deponent, for your use.
Click on this link to begin.

Your final choice should be influenced by the urgency of the need to publish, the type of contract chosen, and the price to publish in a particular newspaper.

Contract types are means through which you obtain your target newspaper copy.

We deliver your target copy to your doorstep, in any location across Nigeria and all available destinations in the world, when you choose the home delivery contract type.

You can also locate and purchase your target copy from a local vendor when you choose the buy from vendor contract type.

A target newspaper copy is a copy of the particular newspaper that contains your classified change of name advert publication, for example, with a newspaper publisher, on a particular date.

Publication price varies among available newspapers.

Click on – Start here – and select a newspaper. The updated price to publish with that newspaper is stated beside it.

The title of the name being changed to determines the supporting documents to submit.


Changing from Mr A to Mr B, a court affidavit and valid means of identification are required.


Changing from Miss A to Miss B, a court affidavit and valid means of identification are required.


Changing from Miss A to Mrs B, a marriage certificate and valid means of identification are required.


Rearrangement Of Name, Confirmation Of Name, Correction of Name, Correction of Date of Birth, a court affidavit and valid means of identification are required.


For unique cases such as a change of name for minors, changing from Mrs A to Miss B, and changing from Mrs A to Mrs B, please contact us.

We commit to next-day delivery of target copies, from the date of publication, to all locations within Lagos.

We communicate your target copy delivery date (by email) as soon as your order is processed.

Please note that some publishers publish name adverts every business day of the week, while others publish only on particular days.

Name adverts are published according to the publication schedule of available publishers.
This means that a newspaper with Tue, Thur, stated beside it, publishes name adverts only on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Hence, an advert order that is placed on Friday would have the expected name advert published on Tuesday, the following week.

To have a name advert published on Tuesday, for example, ensure to place the Name Advert order according to the following schedule: before 4:30 PM on Monday for NewsDirect Newspaper, before 3:00 PM on Monday for The Sun Newspaper, before 1:30 PM on Monday for Punch, Business News, Leadership, and Vanguard Newspapers, and before 2:00 PM on Monday for Business Day Newspaper.

Late submissions are moved to the next publication day of your chosen newspaper.

Select a newspaper to reveal its publishing schedule stated beside its cost to publish.

Newspaper reprint is available only on the home delivery contract type.

We communicate the delivery date (by email) once the publisher approves your reprint request and a reprint is done.

Clicking on – Contact – leads you directly to the newspaper reprint section.
Please fill the form correctly, supplying the target copy's date.

We collaborate with our tier 3 logistics partner to deliver target copies to all locations worldwide, once you initiate a contract.
