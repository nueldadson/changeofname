Our Customers

Over 67% of our customers are newlyweds.

Singles will find true love someday.

So, on our Twitter page, we have dedicated Mondays and Thursdays to doing Twitter quotes of selfies — nice selfies of single ladies (sometimes men, though men seldom snap) who are old enough to be married.

Then, periodically, we run exhibitions, not competitions, in the form of Facebook and Twitter ads that are promoted locally and internationally.

We rely solely on the Twitter algorithm to sort tweets and fill our timeline with content from the right demographic for us to choose a person's spectacular selfie to quote, but we dearly hope that more singles will use our exhibition form to join the funfair by themselves.

Our assurance is that through these engaging exhibitions, our quoted selfies will shine brighter.

Who knows? Maybe someday, these ladies will come back to us as newly-wedded customers.
