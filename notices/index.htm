Frequently Asked Questions

Your final choices should be influenced by the urgency of the need to publish, the type of contract chosen, and the price to publish in those particular newspapers.

Contract types are means through which you obtain your target newspaper copies.

We aggregate and deliver your target copies to your doorstep, in any location across Nigeria and all available destinations in the world, when you choose the home delivery contract type.

You can also locate and purchase your target copies from a local vendor when you choose the buy from vendor contract type.

Target newspaper copies are the copies of the two newspapers that contain your new trustee registration advert publication, for example, with your selected newspaper publishers, on particular dates.

Publication price varies among available newspapers.

Click on – Start here – and select a newspaper. The updated price to publish with that newspaper is stated beside it.

The total publication cost is the sum of publication costs for your chosen newspapers.

We commit to next-day delivery of target copies, from the date of publication, to all locations within Lagos.

We communicate your target copies delivery date (by email) as soon as your order is processed.

Please note that some publishers publish trustees adverts every business day of the week, while others publish only on particular days.

Trustees adverts are published according to the publication schedule of available publishers.
This means that a newspaper with Tues, Thur, stated beside it, publishes trustees adverts only on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Hence, an advert order that is placed on Friday would have the expected trustee advert published on Tuesday, the following week.

To have a trustee advert published on Tuesday, for example, ensure to place the advert order before 1:30 PM on Monday for NewsDirect Newspaper, before 1:30 PM on Monday for The Business News Newspaper, before 1:00 PM on Monday for The Sun Newspaper, before 12:30 PM on Monday for Punch Newspaper, before 11:45 AM on Monday for Business Day Newspaper, before 12:30 PM on Monday for Vanguard Newspaper, and before 1:00 PM on Monday for Leadership Newspaper.

Late submissions are moved to the next publication day of your chosen newspaper.

Select a newspaper to reveal its publishing schedule beside the cost to publish.

We collaborate with our tier 3 logistics partner to deliver target copies to all locations worldwide, once you initiate a contract.
